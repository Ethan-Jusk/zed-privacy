Zed – Data Deletion Instructions

Zed respects your privacy. This page explains how you can request deletion of your personal data associated with Facebook Login.

Option 1: Delete via Facebook

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy → Settings.
  3. Select Apps and Websites.
  4. Find Zed Link and click Remove.
  5. Choose Delete all data and confirm.

Option 2: Request Direct Deletion

You can also request deletion by emailing us:

justifuny@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your Facebook full name
  • Your Facebook email address
  • Subject: Zed Link Data Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Facebook User ID
  • Name and email (if provided)
  • Zed Link account data linked to Facebook Login
  • Authentication tokens

All valid data deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
